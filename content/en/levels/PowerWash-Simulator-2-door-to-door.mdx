<Section icon={Trophy} title="PowerWash Simulator 2 door to door Achievement Overview">

The **PowerWash Simulator 2 door to door** achievement is one of the most meticulous challenges in the base game's Career Mode. Unlike standard cleaning tasks where you sweep from top to bottom, this achievement requires a highly specific "clean-first" approach. You must target every single door, handle, and room number across the entire Motel complex before the game registers any other component as "Clean."

Because the Motel is a large, multi-room building, the margin for error is slim. A stray spray hitting a dirty wall or a nearby window frame could accidentally trigger a completion notification for that part, immediately voiding the achievement for that run. Understanding the specific requirements is the first step toward success.

| :--- | :--- |
| **Name** | Door to Door |
| **Associated Job** | Motel (Career Job 24) |
| **Required Parts** | All Doors, Door Handles, and Door Numbers |
| **Difficulty** | Hard / High Precision |
| **Platform Availability** | PC, PS5, Xbox Series X\|S, Switch 2 |
| **Unlock Condition** | Complete specific tasks before any others |

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Achievement Route">

Following a systematic route is essential. If you jump randomly between rooms, you are likely to miss a single door handle or a small number plate. Use this room-by-room progression to ensure every required part is accounted for before you start the main cleaning of the Motel.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Isolate the First Floor">
    Start at the far left of the ground floor. Approach the first door and crouch to get a better angle on the handle. Clean the **Door Number** first, then the **Door Handle**, and finally the **Door** itself.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The 'Edge-In' Technique">
    When cleaning the door face, always spray from the center outward, but stop before you reach the very edge. For the edges, turn your nozzle vertically and use short pulses to clear the remaining grime without hitting the frame.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check the Task List">
    After every two rooms, open your tablet and check the task list. Ensure that only "Doors," "Door Handles," and "Door Numbers" are showing 100% completion. If any other part (like a wall or walkway) shows 1% or higher, be extremely careful.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Ascend to the Second Floor">
    Use the stairs but avoid spraying them. Repeat the same process for every door on the upper balcony. Don't forget the main office door if it is listed as a separate task.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Verification">
    Before cleaning any walls, floors, or signs, verify that the "Door to Door" achievement notification has popped. If it hasn't, you likely missed a single door handle or a number plate on one of the rooms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid the Scissor Lift">
While the scissor lift is great for the Motel's roof, do not use it while attempting the achievement. The platform can block your view of the door numbers, and it's easy to accidentally spray the railing while trying to reach a door.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Find the Last Dirt">

If you've finished the doors and the rest of the Motel but are stuck at 99%, don't panic. The Motel has several "hidden" spots where dirt tends to hide. Use the systematic sweep method to find the final specks and trigger the completion "ding."

<Checklist
  id="motel-last-dirt"
  title="Common Missed Spots at the Motel:"
  items={[
    "Underside of the balcony walkways",
    "Inside the tracks of the sliding windows",
    "Top edges of the room number plates",
    "The very bottom lip of the ground floor walls",
    "Behind the outdoor light fixtures",
    "The inner crevices of the door handles"
  ]}
/>

When a part is almost clean, stop spraying the whole level. Open the task list, select the specific incomplete part, and look for the orange check indicator. This is especially helpful for the **PowerWash Simulator 2 door to door** achievement if you realize you missed one handle but can't see which room it belongs to.

<Callout type="tip" title="Dirt Highlight Pulse">
Don't hold the dirt highlight button down. Pulse it while moving your camera. The "glow" of the dirt is often easier to see as it flashes against the clean surface.
</Callout>
