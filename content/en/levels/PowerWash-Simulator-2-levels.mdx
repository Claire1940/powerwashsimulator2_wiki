<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step: Finding the Last 1% of Dirt">

The most common frustration in **PowerWash Simulator 2 levels** is getting stuck at 99%. The sequel provides several new tools to help you cross the finish line without aimless spraying.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Isolate the Object">
    Open the **Task List** (Tab/Menu) and look for items that are not at 100%. Click the checkbox next to them to highlight the specific object in the world with an orange indicator.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Pulse the Highlight">
    Use the **Dirt Highlight** button frequently. Instead of holding it, pulse it while moving your camera. Look for glowing specks that contrast against the clean surface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check the 'Hidden' Four">
    Ninety percent of missed dirt is located in four places: **Undersides**, **Rear Faces**, **Trim Seams**, and **Inside Wheel Wells**. Crouch or use the abseiling kit to get a new perspective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Narrow the Stream">
    Switch to the **0-degree (Red) nozzle** or the narrowest setting on your Adaptable Nozzle. Trace the edges of the object slowly. Sometimes the dirt is a single pixel wide along a seam.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Multi-Stage Warning">
In levels like the **Public Facility**, some dirt is literally unreachable until you complete the current stage. If you can't find the last speck, check if a new area has just been unlocked.
</Callout>

</Section>
