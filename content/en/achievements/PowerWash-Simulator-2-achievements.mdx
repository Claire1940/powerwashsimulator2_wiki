<Section icon={Target} title="Level-Specific Cleaning Challenges">

As you progress through the career mode of PowerWash Simulator 2, almost every level features a unique challenge that dictates how you must clean. These achievements usually fall into two categories: "Clean This First" or "Clean This Last." Failing to follow the order often requires a full level restart in Free Play mode.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Order-Based">
    Focus on specific objects like lanterns, satellite dishes, or owls before touching anything else in the environment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Restricted Zone">
    Clean entire sections while avoiding a single "forbidden" object, such as the hatch in the Bandstand level.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Environmental">
    Interact with the physics engine, such as rolling a soccer ball down a slide or jumping between pillars.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

In the **Campsite** job, you must focus on the lighting. There are four lanterns scattered around the site. You must ensure all four are 100% clean before the progress bar for any other object (like the tent or table) moves. Similarly, in the **Rock Climbing** level, the pterodactyl is your primary target.

| Level | Achievement | Primary Target | Constraint |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Campsite | **Hit the Lights** | 4 Lanterns | Clean these first |
| Gas Station | **Soccer Slide** | Soccer Ball | Roll down the slide |
| Rock Climbing | **Flying Start** | Pterodactyl | Clean first |
| Car & Trailer | **Message Received** | Satellite Dish | Clean first |
| Shopping Mall | **Wise Decision** | Owl Statue | Clean first |

<Callout type="tip" title="Soap Strategy">
For the **Doggy Bath** achievement, you need the Prime Vista 2400 soap nozzle. The trick is to cover the entire car in soap *before* any cleaning progress is made. If the achievement doesn't pop, check the wheel wells and the undercarriage for tiny missed spots.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Advanced Physics and Interaction Mastery">

PowerWash Simulator 2 introduces more complex environmental puzzles than its predecessor. Some achievements require platforming skills or precise physics manipulation. The "Floor is Lava" achievement in the Stone Circle level is a prime example of this, requiring you to navigate the entire perimeter without touching the ground.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Jump">
    Start on the raised wooden platform near the entrance of the Stone Circle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Pillar Navigation">
    Jump to the first stone pillar and continue clockwise. You must land firmly on the top surface of each pillar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Leap of Faith">
    The gap between the fourth and fifth pillar is the widest. Use a sprint-jump to ensure you clear the distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Stretch">
    Complete the circle by landing on the final pillar. The achievement **Floor is Lava** should pop immediately upon landing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Another tricky interaction is found in the **Teapot** level. You must locate six hidden gnomes and place them specifically in the chairs surrounding the table. In earlier versions, this was buggy, so ensure the gnomes are seated upright. Once they are seated, take one gnome onto the lift and raise it to the maximum height to trigger the **Rise and Shine** achievement.

<Callout type="info" title="Physics Reset">
If you accidentally launch the soccer ball off the map at the Gas Station, simply quit to the main menu and reload the job. The ball will reset to its original position, allowing you to try the slide maneuver again.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Strict Order and Precision Cleaning">

The most difficult **PowerWash Simulator 2 achievements** are those that require "surgical" precision. The **Motel** and **Theater** levels are notorious for this. In the Theater, you must clean the entire front row of seats—including the bars and under-seat areas—without completing the cleaning of the floor or the rows behind it.

In the **Motel** level, the challenge is even stricter. You must clean all six doors (including handles and room numbers) while strictly avoiding the door frames.

| Level | Achievement | Goal | Forbidden Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bandstand | **Hatching a Plan** | Clean the floor hatch last | Cleaning hatch before 99% |
| Theater | **Front Row Seats** | Clean row 1 first | Completing floor/row 2 |
| Motel | **Room Service** | Clean doors/handles | Touching the door frames |
| Town Float | **Squeaky Clean** | Clean the mouse last | Completing mouse body early |

<Callout type="success" title="Mouse Strategy">
When working on the **Squeaky Clean** achievement at the Town Float, switch to the smallest 0-degree nozzle when cleaning near the mouse. The mouse consists of the body, two ears, the head, and the tail. Check your job list frequently to ensure these five items are the only ones left before finishing.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Solar System and Equipment Completion">

The **Solar System** level is widely considered the ultimate test of patience. To unlock the associated achievement, you must clean the Sun first, followed by the planets in their specific orbital order from the Sun. Missing a single moon or core component will invalidate the run.

| Order | Celestial Body | Key Components to Check |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **The Sun** | Entire surface area |
| 2 | **Mercury** | Crust and surface |
| 3 | **Venus** | Atmosphere and surface |
| 4 | **Earth** | Crust, Inner Core, Mantle, Outer Core |
| 5 | **Mars** | Both moons (Deimos/Phobos) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Earth Warning">
The Earth is the most common point of failure. You must clean the Inner Core, Outer Core, Mantle, and Super Plume Tuso. The pedestal, the sign, and the Moon arm do *not* count toward the planet's completion and should be left for the very end.
</Callout>

After completing the career mode, you will likely have accumulated over $30,000. This is the time to head to the shop and finalize your equipment achievements. Purchasing every washer and every corresponding attachment is required for the **Maxed Out** achievement.
