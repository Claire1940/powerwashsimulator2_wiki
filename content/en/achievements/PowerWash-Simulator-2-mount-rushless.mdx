<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Cleaning Walkthrough">

To conquer **PowerWash Simulator 2 mount rushless** without losing your mind, you must divide the monument into manageable segments. Cleaning randomly will lead to "completion fatigue" where you have 98% finished but cannot find the remaining specks across the massive map.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Top-Down Abseiling Pass">
    Start at the very top of the monument using the abseiling rig. Clean the hair and the top of the heads first. This prevents "phantom runoff" and ensures you aren't pushing dirt onto areas you've already cleaned below.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Facial Features">
    Move down to the eyes, nose, and mouth. These are the most complex parts of the job. Use the 15-degree nozzle or the Urban X Double Turbo to get into the deep recesses. Ensure the "inner ear" tasks are 100% before moving lower.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Broad Surface Sweep">
    Switch to the Prime Vista Pro with the Triple-Tip nozzle. Sweep across the cheeks, necks, and chest areas. These are the largest tasks by surface area and provide the fastest percentage gains.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Scaffolding and Support">
    As you descend, clean the wooden scaffolding and metal supports. These are often forgotten but count toward the total job completion. Use the scissor lift to reach the underside of the platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Base and Surroundings">
    Finish with the ground-level rocks and the commemorative plaques at the bottom. By the time you reach the floor, the top 80% of the monument should already be glowing white on your task list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Co-op Efficiency">
Mount Rushless supports up to 4 players online. In co-op, assign one player to each face. This allows you to tackle the most detailed sections simultaneously, cutting the total completion time by nearly 70%.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Finding the Last 1% of Dirt">

The most common frustration with **PowerWash Simulator 2 mount rushless** is getting stuck at 99%. On a map this size, a single dirty pebble or a thin line of grime in a nose crease can prevent the final completion "ding."

  id="rushless-completion"
  title="Commonly Missed Spots:"

To isolate these spots, open your **Object List** and select the tasks that are not at 100%. The game will pulse an orange marker over the unfinished objects. If the marker points to a face you thought was done, use the **Dirt Highlight** (TAB on PC) while standing on the scissor lift to see the glowing orange specks.

| Problem | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Stuck at 99.8%** | Check the 'Commemorative Plaque' at the base |
| **Cannot reach top** | Reset the abseiling rig to the highest anchor |
| **Grime won't budge** | Switch to the Red 0-degree nozzle or use Soap |
| **Object not found** | Highlight the task in the menu to see the orange icon |

<Callout type="info" title="Visual Indicators">
PowerWash Simulator 2 has improved the orange task markers. If you are far away, the marker will appear as a solid icon. As you get closer, it fades, helping you pinpoint the exact square inch of dirt remaining.
</Callout>
