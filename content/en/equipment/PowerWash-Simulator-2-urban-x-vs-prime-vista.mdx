<Section icon={Target} title="Advanced Cleaning Strategies & FAQ">

When tackling massive late-game locations, efficiency is key. Always work from top to bottom so that dirty runoff does not ruin clean surfaces below. Use your dirt highlight pulse frequently to spot hidden specks of grime along seams, trim borders, and corners. If you find yourself stuck at 99% completion, open your task list, select the incomplete component, and look for the orange check indicators in the world to locate the remaining dirt.

For stubborn, layered grime, do not hesitate to utilize **Soap 2.0**. Soap is completely free to use in this sequel and clings visibly to dirty surfaces. Coat a heavily soiled area with the Soap Nozzle first to soften the grime, then rinse it clean with a high-pressure water nozzle. This technique is incredibly effective when cleaning heavy vehicles like the Mining Dump Truck or the 18 Wheeler.
