<Section icon={Swords} title="PowerWash Simulator 2 How to Equip New Equipment: Step-by-Step">

Purchasing gear is only the first step. To use your new tools on a job, you must access your inventory and update your active loadout. This process can be done at the start of any contract or mid-job if you need to swap attachments to reach high places or clean stubborn grime.

<Callout type="warning" title="Job Restrictions">
Some specialized career missions limit the types of equipment you can bring. Always check the job details before purchasing expensive upgrades specifically for a single mission.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open the Inventory Menu">
    Press the inventory key on your platform while on a active job site to bring up your loadout customization screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select the Equipment Category">
    Navigate through the tabs to find the specific category you want to modify, such as Washers, Extensions, or Nozzles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Your Purchased Gear">
    Scroll through your owned inventory items and select the newly purchased pressure washer or attachment to highlight it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm and Equip">
    Click the equip button or press the corresponding controller button to set the selected item as your active tool, then close the menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
