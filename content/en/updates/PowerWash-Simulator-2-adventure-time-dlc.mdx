<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Treehouse Cleaning Guide">

The Treehouse is the most complex level due to its multi-layered structure and the sheer variety of "slob" debris left by Finn and Jake. Follow these steps to maximize your efficiency and ensure you don't miss hidden spots under the pizza boxes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Exterior Trunk Foundation">
    Start from the roots and work your way up the main trunk. Use the 25-degree nozzle to strip the thickest mud layers before moving to the branches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Kitchen & Pizza Party Area">
    Focus on the floor first. The pizza grease requires the "Yellow Nozzle" or soap. Be sure to clean the undersides of the table where Jake hides his snacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Finn and Jake's Bedroom">
    This area is cluttered. Use the "Red Nozzle" for the precise corners of the bunk beds. Don't forget to spray the ceiling fan, which is often coated in dust.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Lookout Balcony">
    The highest point of the level. Use the long-range extension to reach the outer leaves. Watch for hidden "Gunter" graffiti on the telescope.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Cleaning Phase | Recommended Nozzle | Surface Material |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Heavy Mud** | 15-Degree (Yellow) | Wood / Bark |
| **Pizza Grease** | Soap / 25-Degree | Floor Tiles |
| **Delicate Items** | 40-Degree (White) | BMO / Electronics |
| **Tight Corners** | 0-Degree (Red) | Metal Joints |

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Cleaning BMO requires extreme care. Use the White Nozzle to avoid "damaging" the character, though the game won't actually let you break him.
</Callout>
