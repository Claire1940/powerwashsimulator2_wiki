</Callout>

The upcoming Bouncy Castle job promises to introduce vertical physics never before seen in the series. Players will have to time their sprays mid-air while bouncing to reach the highest points of the inflatable structure.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Quality of Life & System Improvements">

Every major PowerWash Simulator 2 update focuses heavily on community feedback. The developers have actively patched structural bugs, improved performance across all platforms, and added highly requested features to make the cleaning experience smoother.

The introduction of Update 1.2 and Update 1.3 brought several critical quality-of-life adjustments that change how players manage their equipment and track their progress.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Utilize the Message Archive">
    Open your in-game menu and navigate to the new Message Archive. Added in Update 1.3, this feature allows you to sort incoming messages by job or group chat, making it much easier to follow the narrative threads of Caldera County.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Stack Home Base Furniture">
    Return to your Home Base and access the decoration menu. Following Update 1.2, you can now stack selected furniture items. This allows for far more creative layouts and personalized headquarters designs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Controller Deadzones">
    If playing on console or with a gamepad, head to the controller settings. Use the newly added deadzone sliders to fine-tune your aiming precision, which is crucial for hitting tiny missed spots on complex machinery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Achievement Tracking Fix">
Update 1.3 drastically improved the reliability of "clean-first" and "clean-last" achievements. If you previously had trouble unlocking achievements like *Hatching a Plan* or *Squeaky Clean*, replay the jobs in Free Play mode; the triggers are now highly stable.
</Callout>

Additionally, developers resolved several multiplayer connectivity issues. If you frequently experienced desyncs during 4-player online co-op sessions, the netcode improvements in the latest patch should provide a much more stable connection.

</Section>
