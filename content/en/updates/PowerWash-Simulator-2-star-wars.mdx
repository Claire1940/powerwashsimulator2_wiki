<Section icon={Target} title="Campaign Levels and Strategic Cleaning Routes">

To maximize efficiency and earn five-star ratings on every job, you must approach each level with a clear plan. The verticality of these levels means that starting from the bottom will only lead to dirty water dripping down onto already cleaned sections. Always work from the highest point downward, clearing large flat surfaces before tackling detailed components.

This top-down methodology is especially important when dealing with the Millennium Falcon and the X-wing. The sloped hulls of these ships naturally channel water and loose grime downward. If you clean the landing gear first, you will find yourself wasting time re-cleaning them once you wash the upper fuselage.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimal Tool Selection and Nozzle Configurations">

Selecting the correct nozzle is the difference between a quick, satisfying clean and hours of tedious spraying. The Star Wars vehicles feature varying types of dirt, from light space dust to baked-on carbon scoring. Matching the right pressure and spray angle to the surface material is crucial.

Using the wrong nozzle can result in wasted time. For instance, using the white 40-degree nozzle on stubborn carbon burns will do almost nothing, while using the red 0-degree nozzle on large flat wings will take hours to cover the surface area. Learn to switch nozzles dynamically as you move between different parts of the ship.

| Nozzle Type | Spray Angle | Ideal Surface | Cleaning Speed |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Red Nozzle** | 0 Degrees | Deep rust, carbon burns | **Very Slow** |
| **Yellow Nozzle** | 15 Degrees | Metal plating, heavy dirt | **Medium** |
| **Green Nozzle** | 25 Degrees | Standard paint, dust | **Fast** |
| **White Nozzle** | 40 Degrees | Large flat wings, glass | **Very Fast** |
| **Soap Nozzle** | Wide Spray | Pre-treating grease | **Instant Spread** |

To optimize your workflow, organize your cleaning process into three distinct phases using different equipment configurations:
