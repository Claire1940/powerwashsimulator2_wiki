<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Recommended Gear and Cleaning Steps">

Succeeding in the Adventure Time expansion requires selecting the appropriate washer configuration. Standard equipment works, but utilizing the new themed gear sets optimizes your cleaning speed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select the Right Washer and Extension">
    Equip the Heavy-Duty Power Washer with a long extension rod. This setup allows you to reach high branches on the Treehouse and tall turrets on the Candy Castle without constantly moving scaffolding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Apply Specialized Cleaning Liquids">
    Use the newly introduced Sweet-Dissolve Soap for the Candy Kingdom maps. This liquid quickly breaks down sticky sugar glaze, reducing the time spent scrubbing delicate structures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clean from Top to Bottom">
    Always start cleaning from the highest point of the structure. This prevents dirty runoff water from flowing down and ruining areas you have already washed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Perform Detail Work with Narrow Nozzles">
    Switch to the 15-degree or 0-degree nozzle for stubborn grime spots, especially around the intricate carvings of the princess jail and guest rooms.
  </Step>
</Steps>
