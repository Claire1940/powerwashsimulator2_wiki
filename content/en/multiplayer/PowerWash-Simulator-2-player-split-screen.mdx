<Section icon={Target} title="How to Setup PowerWash Simulator 2 2 Player Split Screen">

Setting up a local session is straightforward, but it requires navigating the multiplayer menu correctly before launching a job. Because the game tracks complex cleaning data for thousands of surface "voxels," the host's hardware manages the physics for both players simultaneously.

Follow these steps to initiate your **PowerWash Simulator 2 2 player split screen** session:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Multiplayer Menu">
    From the main menu or the pause screen within the Home Base, select the **Multiplayer** tab. Ensure your second controller is powered on and synced to the system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Toggle Local Co-op">
    Switch the session type from "Singleplayer" to "Local Split-Screen." The game will prompt the second player to press a specific button (usually 'Options' or 'Start') to join the lobby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Your Loadout">
    Both players can visit the Equipment Shop to customize their washers. Player 2 will have access to the same tier of equipment unlocked by the host's Career progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Launch the Job">
    Open the Job Map and select any available assignment. The game will load the environment and split the display once both players are ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Performance Tip">
If you experience frame rate drops on older hardware during split-screen, try disabling "High Quality Water Reflections" in the video settings to stabilize the output.
</Callout>

</Section>
