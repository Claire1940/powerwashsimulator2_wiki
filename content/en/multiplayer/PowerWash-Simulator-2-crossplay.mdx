<Section icon={Settings} title="How to Enable and Setup Multiplayer Sessions">

Setting up a session for **PowerWash Simulator 2 crossplay** is a straightforward process, but it requires the host to manually enable online features. By default, many sessions start in "Solo" mode to preserve performance and prevent unwanted interruptions. To bring friends into your world, you must navigate to the multi-functional social menu located within the tablet interface or the main pause screen.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Social Menu">
    Launch your desired cleaning job in Career or Free Play mode. Once the level loads, press the Escape key (PC) or the Options/Menu button (Console). Look for the icon that resembles two people standing together.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Toggle Online Multiplayer">
    In the top right corner of the social overlay, you will see a toggle for "Online Multiplayer." Switch this to the "On" position. This action initializes the game server for your specific session.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Generate and Copy Room Code">
    Once online, a "Room Code" field will appear. Click the "Copy" button next to the code. This unique alphanumeric string is what your friends on other platforms will need to join your game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Share the Code">
    Send the copied code to your friends via Discord, text, or platform-specific messaging. They must enter this code in the "Join a Game" section of their own main menu to connect to your instance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Session Visibility">
Enabling **PowerWash Simulator 2 crossplay** via Room Codes does not make your game public to the entire world. Only players with your specific code can join, ensuring your private cleaning sessions remain secure from random players.
</Callout>
