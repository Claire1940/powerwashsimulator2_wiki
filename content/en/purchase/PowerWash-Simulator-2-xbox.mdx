<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Set Up Xbox Co-op Multiplayer">

Cleaning is more satisfying when shared with friends. The game supports online cooperative play, allowing multiple players to tackle massive cleaning jobs together. Setting up a session requires configuring the in-game multiplayer settings and inviting players through the Xbox Network.

Co-op mode scales the cleaning progress based on the number of players in the game. However, note that career progression is only saved for the host player, while joining players receive monetary compensation for their work.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open the Tablet Menu">
    Press the View button on your Xbox controller to open the in-game tablet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Toggle Co-op Status">
    Navigate to the top right corner of the screen and toggle the Co-op switch to 'On'.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Matchmaking Type">
    Choose between 'Friends Only' or 'Crossplay' depending on who you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Send Invites">
    Select 'Invite Friends' to open the Xbox overlay and select players from your friends list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Crossplay Enabled">
Crossplay must be enabled in the main settings menu before starting a job if you want to join players on PC.
</Callout>

| Game Mode | Player Limit | Crossplay Support | Progression Saved |
| Career Mode | 2 Players | Enabled | Host Only |
| Free Play | 6 Players | Enabled | None |
| Special Jobs | 6 Players | Enabled | None |

When playing in larger groups, coordination is key. Assigning specific sections of a map to each player prevents redundant cleaning and ensures that soap nozzles are utilized efficiently on appropriate surfaces.
